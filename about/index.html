Hello!

My name is Eric Yager. I’m a senior computer science and math double major at Valparaiso University, but I graduate this May. During my time at Valpo, I’ve served as treasurer and (currently) president of Valpo’s student chapter of the Association for Computing Machinery (ACM). I’ve also been vice president of Valpo’s Math Club for the last two years. Additionally, I help students better understand computer science course content as a teaching assistant, a job I started in 2019.

Some of my hobbies include playing chess (I’m especially fond of the bughouse variant), reading, programming projects in Go, and tent camping with friends.

If you’re interested in seeing my work, check my GitHub.
