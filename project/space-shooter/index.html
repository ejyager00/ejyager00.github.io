Space Shooter

During the Fall 2020, Spring 2021 school year, Valparaiso University’s student chapter of the Association for Computing Machinery (of which I was vice president) worked on a team project to develop a video game. Ultimately, we decided to use Python and PyGame to make the project more accessible to members with little programming experience. We decided to make a space-themed endless third person shooter.
